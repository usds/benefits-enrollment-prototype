Use this list to help you gather what you may need to apply.

Here’s some info we need:

  • You and your household, addresses, and date of birth
  • Phone number to follow-up, which helps you get benefits quicker
  • Money from jobs or other sources, and employer info
  • Housing expenses
  • Social Security Numbers for anyone you’re applying for
  • Government benefits such as unemployment or retirement account payments
  • Any medical coverage, disabilities, and/or pregnancies
Info for immigrants

Applying for these programs will not affect your or your family's immigration status.

If you or someone in your household is not a citizen, you may still be able to receive some benefits.

We ask about citizenship so that we can help you find out what programs you qualify for.

This information is kept confidential and secure as required by law. Learn more.
