You do not have to be a citizen to qualify for all benefits.

Answering these questions will not affect your immigration status.

Is everyone in your household a U.S. citizen or U.S. national?
Why do we ask this?

If you or someone in your household is not a citizen, you may still be able to receive some benefits. We ask about citizenship so that we can help you find out what programs you qualify for.

Applying for these programs will not affect your or your family's immigration status.

This information is private and kept confidential. It will not be shared with any agency for any reason other than checking your eligibility.

To sign up for these services, we may need to ask for immigration document numbers.
